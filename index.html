About RIT CyberGuard

RIT CyberGuard is the newly established cybersecurity club at Ramco Institute of Technology (RIT), located in Rajapalayam, Tamil Nadu. As we begin our journey in 2025, we're building a community of passionate students dedicated to cybersecurity excellence.

About Ramco Institute of Technology

  • Established in 2013 by the Ramco Group
  • Located in Rajapalayam, Tamil Nadu, India
  • Affiliated with Anna University, Chennai
  • Approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)
  • Vision: Providing high-quality, affordable, and world-class engineering education

Our Mission

To build cybersecurity awareness and skills among students from all engineering disciplines, creating a supportive learning environment where beginners can grow into cybersecurity professionals. We welcome students regardless of their experience level.
